More — Vonnegut's Refrain

Minimalist phrasing, maximal effect.

Quote

“So it goes.”

Meaning & context

Vonnegut's short phrase normalizes and distances occurrences of death. As a recurrent refrain it shapes the novel's tone and structure, registering loss with a resigned cadence.

Quick notes

  • Repeated after mentions of death.
  • Signals the book's fatalistic and anti-war themes.
