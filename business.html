Business — Orwell on Power & Language

How slogans and language can reshape reality.

Quote

“All animals are equal, but some animals are more equal than others.”

Meaning & context

Orwell's paradox is a satirical emblem of political doublespeak — a concise demonstration of how language can be used to legitimize inequality and mask power dynamics.

Quick notes

  • Represents final corruption of revolutionary ideals on the farm.
  • Example of how slogans become tools of control.
